We are a group of students studying at TARUC, Kuala Lumpur Malaysia
in 2022, when our team worked together to make an online
clothing store. Thus, the predecessor of Fresh Fit was born.
Founded by 5 college student who aspire to be graphic designers.
Classy, Sophisticated and Dazzling -- our product represents
each and every of our own craftsmanship which focuses on quality
and attention to detail. Our target audience are young people,
so our clothing design are modern, stylish and picked to *Fit* freshly.
Hence the name, Fresh Fit.

  • What are our products made of?

    • We use a lot of organic cotton to create our clothing as it's soft, breathable and comfy which are also environmentally friendly.

  • Are the shirts/pants expensive?

    • We deliver our business concept – fashion and quality at the best price – to everyone, and we do so in a sustainable way.

  • Can I get a refund if I purchase the wrong size?

    • Yes! You can go to our website and contact the customer service about the problems you are facing.

  • What's the plan for the future?

    • We plan to expand our business globally one day and by creating the best product which fits our customer's standard.
